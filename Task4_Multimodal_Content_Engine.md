## Task 4: The Multimodal Content Engine

Objective

To design a multimodal AI workflow capable of converting long-form video content into multiple short-form social media assets. The workflow combines speech-to-text transcription, content analysis, viral segment identification, caption generation, and B-roll suggestions.

⸻

Problem Statement

Content creators spend significant time manually reviewing videos, identifying highlights, creating captions, and editing short-form clips for social media platforms.

The goal of this project is to automate this process using AI-powered multimodal tools.

⸻

Tools Used

* Whisper (Speech-to-Text)
* GPT-4o
* Runway
* Prompt Engineering

⸻

Workflow Overview

Step 1: Video Input

Input: 10-minute YouTube video

The source video is uploaded into the workflow for processing.

⸻

Step 2: Audio Transcription

Whisper is used to convert speech into text.

Sample Transcript

“Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries by automating repetitive tasks, improving decision-making, and enabling businesses to scale more efficiently.”

⸻

Step 3: Viral Segment Detection

GPT-4o analyzes the transcript and identifies the most engaging moments based on:

* Emotional impact
* Educational value
* Audience retention potential
* Social media shareability

⸻
